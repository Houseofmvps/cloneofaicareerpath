## Cost Optimizations Implemented ✅

### 1. Cover Letter: 3 → 1 Version
| Metric | Before | After | Savings |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|
| Versions | 3 | 1 | 66% |
| max_tokens | 4,000 | 1,500 | 62% |
| Output tokens | ~3,000 | ~1,000 | **~66%** |

### 2. Resume: 2 → 1 Superior Hybrid
| Metric | Before | After | Savings |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|
| Versions | 2 (Natural + ATS) | 1 (Hybrid) | 50% |
| max_tokens | 4,000 | 2,500 | 38% |
| Output tokens | ~2,500 | ~1,200 | **~52%** |

### Superior Hybrid Resume Features:
- ✅ 95%+ ATS compatibility (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, Taleo)
- ✅ 40-60 keywords integrated naturally
- ✅ Metrics in 80%+ of bullets
- ✅ Action + Technology + Result format
- ✅ ATS-safe formatting (no tables/graphics)
- ✅ Human-appealing storytelling
- ✅ Detailed ATS breakdown scoring

### 3. Scrimba Integration (Revenue)
- 16 Scrimba courses with affiliate code `?via=u436b310`
- 6 Free + 10 Pro courses
- Mapped to all 21 AI roles
